Reporting content or another user

Cover Point has no tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users. In the app, open a player's profile card from the Pavilion and use:

  • Report — flag an offensive name, offensive content, harassment or spam. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
  • Block — stop that player finding you, seeing your match stories, or viewing your profile. You can unblock any time in Profile → Settings.

You can also email us at the address above and we will act on it.

Common questions

I logged a match but it hasn't appeared for my club.

Check the match was tagged to your club when you saved it. Open the match from Profile → Match book, tap edit, and set "Share to club".

I had no signal at the ground. Did I lose my match?

No. Matches save to your phone and sync automatically when you're back online. A match waiting to sync shows "NOT SYNCED" on its card, and you can force it with Profile → Settings → Sync now.

My season stats look wrong.

Check your season start month in Edit profile. UK seasons normally start in April; southern-hemisphere seasons start in October and are labelled across two years, e.g. 2026/27. Club leaderboards use the club's own setting, which a captain can change.

How do I join a club?

Ask a club captain for the invite code, then enter it on the Club tab. Captains can find the code at the top of their club page.

How do I delete my account?

Profile → Delete my account. This permanently removes your profile and all of your matches and cannot be undone.

Can I get a copy of my data?

Yes. Email us and we'll send you an export of everything we hold.

Reporting a bug

Email us with your device (e.g. iPhone 14), what you were doing, and what happened. A screenshot helps enormously.
